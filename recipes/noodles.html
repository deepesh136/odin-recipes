Veg Hakka Noodles

Veg hakka noodles is quick and easy to make at home. Learn how to make a perfect, flavor packed recipe at home that will satisfy your street food cravings.

Description

Quick and easy to make vegetable Hakka noodles recipe. Tossing in high flame so that it catches fire gives a smoky flavor just like the the street food / restaurants style

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Plain noodles
  • ¾ Cabbage Shredded / julienne
  • 1 Carrot Medium
  • ½ Capsicum
  • 3 Sprigs Spring onion
  • ½ teaspoon Pepper
  • 1.5 teaspoon Tomato chilli sauce
  • Salt
  • 3 tablespoon Oil

Steps

  1. Cook noodles as per the instructions given in the pack
  2. Boil 4 cups of water and cook noodles for 3-4 minutes. Make sure its not over cooked.
  3. Drain water and wash it to remove the excess starch optionally. Reserve som noodles cooked water (2-3 tbsp) to use later.
  4. Add 1 teaspoon oil and mix to avoid sticking.
  5. Cut veggies into fine thin strips, chop the spring onion finely (green part) and chop the white part separately.
  6. Heat a pan with oil and fry white part of the spring onion.
  7. Add the veggies one by one frying each for half minute. Add the sauce, salt and pepper. Give a quick stir.
  8. Add the drained noodles and mix well. If too sticky, or if you want to deglaze the pan, add the reserved pasta cooked water.
  9. Finally add the chopped spring onion to garnish.
